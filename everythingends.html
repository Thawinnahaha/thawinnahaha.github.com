Everything Ends

A song that even reached the metal charts #1 position

Songstructure

“Everything Ends” by Architects is built around a tense, ascending structure that moves from tightly wound, rhythmic verses into explosive, emotionally charged choruses marked by sharp riffs and urgent vocals.

Information

The track was written during a period of mounting personal and internal struggles within the band, with themes that later grew even more poignant in light of guitarist Tom Searle's battle with cancer and the group's confrontation with grief and impermanence.

R.I.P Tom Searle
